AI4RA UDM should be presented as the ecosystem's semantic foundation.

Its purpose is not simply technical standardization. It helps institutions describe and
translate their work consistently enough to support reporting, interoperability, and
shared tooling without erasing local realities.

Each release page should answer:

- why the community needs this
- what institutional problems it addresses
- what governance or stewardship is required
- how practitioners and implementers can engage
